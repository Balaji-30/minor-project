About Us

A minor project done by the 6th semester CSE students Balaji S (4NI18CS014),H G Saatvik (4NI18CS025),Harisha S (4NI18CS026) from The National Institute of Engineering. Under the guidance of Mrs. Ramya S.

The average human functions at optimum always when he/she does one thing at a time with full focus. The mind can often be bogged down by the thought of multiple tasks of varying importance that remain to be completed during the day. To avoid this conundrum, many people hire assistants or secretaries to help with scheduling and managerial needs. However in this age of automation, the ideal solution is to develop a task manager that acts as a virtual assistant to the human, simplifying the whole exercise of planning your day!

Our solution is an integrated task manager application that can help effectively manage tasks throughout the day. The application is packed with features that will help you to keep track and assist with scheduling without any hassles. We hope you enjoy using our application and find it useful for everyday tasks. This project was built using python's django framework, SqlLite, Bootstrap 5 and html/css. Thank you for visiting.
